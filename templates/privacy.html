Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and display your personal expense records.
  • To generate summaries and breakdowns of your spending.
  • To send transactional emails (e.g., password resets) if applicable.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or unaffiliated third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply industry-standard measures including encrypted storage and access controls to protect your information. Despite these measures, no system is completely immune to risk — use a strong, unique password for your account.

You may request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are bound by data processing agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other sites.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or wish to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out through the app's support channel. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
